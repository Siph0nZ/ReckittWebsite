  • Aids healing and moistures

Bonjela Cold Sore Cream

Bonjela Cold Sore Cream effectively treats cold sores and contains UV-Active™ which creates a protective film layer on the skin cells of the cold sore and prevents attachment of virus particles to the cells. It also​ protects the skin against harmful environmental triggers of the herpes simplex virus such as UV radiation.

Symptoms Treated:
  • Itching and Burning
  • Pain
 Available Sizes:
  • 2g/2ml
